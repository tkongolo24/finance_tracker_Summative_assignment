About Student Finance Tracker

The Student Finance Tracker is designed to help students take control of their personal finances. It allows you to track your expenses, set monthly budgets, visualize spending patterns, and make smarter financial decisions — all in one intuitive dashboard.

Our Mission

To empower students to manage money confidently and build healthy financial habits through a simple, accessible, and powerful tool.

Key Features

  • 💰 Track and categorize transactions
  • 📊 Visual spending analytics and charts
  • 🎯 Set and monitor your monthly budget
  • ☁️ Save and load your data locally
  • 🌗 Light / Dark mode toggle
